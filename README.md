## What Praeco Does

Most app founders spend months building and minutes on their launch. They ship without clear positioning, paste the same copy across every channel, and wonder why nobody shows up.

Praeco fixes that.

Founders fill in one structured form — app name, category, audience, problem, differentiator, tone, platform, price model, launch stage — and Praeco generates:

- 5 positioning one-liners across 5 angles (Benefit-led, Problem-first, Audience-first, Outcome-focused, Emotion-led)
- App Store description written to convert
- 5 tweets across every launch format
- Reddit post with the right subreddit tags
- Product Hunt tagline under 60 characters
- LinkedIn post
- 3 email subject line variations
- Press hook written like a TechCrunch opener

All in under 60 seconds. One click exports the full kit as .txt.

## Target Audience

Solo app founders, indie makers, and first-time launchers who need professional launch copy without hiring a copywriter. Primarily developers and product builders who ship fast and need their marketing to keep up.

---

## Target Countries

United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, India — English-speaking markets where app launches on the App Store and Google Play are most competitive and where indie hacker and Product Hunt culture is strongest.

## Competitors

- **Copy.ai / Jasper** — general AI writing tools, not launch-specific
- **Appfigures** — ASO focused, no multi-channel copy kit
- **AppFollow** — review management, not launch copy
- **Manual copywriters** — expensive, slow, not accessible to solo founders

---

## Our Advantage

Praeco is the only tool built specifically around the launch moment. It starts with positioning — not copy — because great copy without clear positioning is just noise. Every piece of the 7-channel kit is anchored to one locked core message, keeping everything consistent and on-brand. It's fully static, no login, no backend, no friction. Founders go from zero to a complete launch kit in under 60 seconds.
