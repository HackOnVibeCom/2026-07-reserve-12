# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

Praeco is an AI-powered promotional copy kit for newly launched mobile apps. Founders fill in one structured form (app name, category, audience, problem, differentiator, tone, platform, price model, launch stage) and Praeco generates a full launch kit in under 60 seconds: 5 positioning one-liners across 5 angles (Benefit-led, Problem-first, Audience-first, Outcome-focused, Emotion-led), an App Store description written to convert, 5 tweets across every launch format, a Reddit post with the right subreddit tags, a Product Hunt tagline under 60 characters, a LinkedIn post, 3 email subject-line variations, and a press hook written like a TechCrunch opener. One click exports the full kit as .txt.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Solo app founders, indie makers, and first-time launchers who need professional launch copy without hiring a copywriter — primarily developers and product builders who ship fast and need their marketing to keep up.

**3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**

United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and India — English-speaking markets where App Store and Google Play launches are most competitive and where indie hacker and Product Hunt culture is strongest.

**4. Who are your competitors?**

Copy.ai / Jasper (general AI writing tools, not launch-specific); Appfigures (ASO focused, no multi-channel copy kit); AppFollow (review management, not launch copy); and manual copywriters (expensive, slow, and not accessible to solo founders).

**5. What is your advantage?**

Praeco is the only tool built specifically around the launch moment. It starts with positioning — not copy — because great copy without clear positioning is just noise. Every piece of the 7-channel kit is anchored to one locked core message, keeping everything consistent and on-brand. It's fully static with no login and no backend, so founders go from zero to a complete launch kit in under 60 seconds.
